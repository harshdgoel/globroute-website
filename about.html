About Globroute Ventures LLP

India's trusted partner for premium Ayurvedic exports

Our Mission

At  Globroute Trade, we are committed to connecting India’s finest products with the world. Headquartered in India, we are a growing export-import company specializing in the global trade of premium Organic Ayurvedic herbs & processed foods including Psyllium Husk Powder, Ashwagandha, Moringa, Neem, Onion and Garlic powder, Red chillies (dried) and other products rooted in centuries-old Indian tradition.

Our journey began with a mission: to deliver authentic, sustainably sourced herbal products from the heart of India to international markets with integrity, transparency, and care. We ensure every product meets global quality and compliance standards.

But Globroute is more than just herbs.

We are building a global route — a trusted channel for a wide range of Indian goods to reach worldwide customers. With scalable logistics, a strong supplier network, and a future-ready outlook, we’re continuously exploring new product categories such as organic foods, textiles, and wellness essentials.

As we grow, our promise remains simple: to bridge borders through ethical trade and to represent the richness of Indian quality and culture on a global stage.

Let’s grow global, together.
